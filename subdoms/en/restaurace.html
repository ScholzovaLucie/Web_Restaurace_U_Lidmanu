In the restaurant with a fireplace and a capacity of 40 people, we serve classic dishes of Czech cuisine and homemade desserts in the form of DAILY OFFER. Our bar offers Moravian wines, regional beers, soft, alcoholic and hot drinks.
In pleasant weather you can sit in our summer teracce. Here you can find our old apple tree. It was chosen as the Czech Tree of the Year in 2020. Here you can enjoy the peace of the countryside and the beautiful view of the surrounding hills. Children can play in the sandbox or have fun on the swing.
The lounge of the restaurant, with a capacity of 20 people, is used for organizing small celebrations, as a training room, as a social room for overnight guests or in case of a larger number of guests to expand the restaurant.
A large social hall with a capacity of 70 people is intended for organizing larger social events - weddings, celebrations, trainings, lectures, exhibitions, etc.
