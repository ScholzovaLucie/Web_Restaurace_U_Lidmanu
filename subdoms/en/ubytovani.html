Our pension offers accommodation in five rooms with private bathroom, with a total capacity of 13 beds and 3 fixed extra beds. The rooms are furnished with antique country furniture (however, the beds have been adapted to today's requirements for a healthy sleep), with high quality bed linen, towels and free wireless internet access. In the common areas of the pension you will also find a refrigerator, microwave oven and kettle.
For relaxation you can use the common room with TV, board games, library and children's corner. You can also play table tennis in the large hall. In summer you can enjoy the evenings on the terrace of our restaurant.

Additional information about your stay

  • The whole pension is non-smoking.
  • Parking is free of charge at the pension.
  • BY PRIOR ARRANGEMENT you can also bring your dog for walks in the nature.
  • We will store your bicycles, skis or strollers at the pension.
  • On request we will be happy to clean the rooms during your stay.

Catering

The price of accommodation includes a buffet BREAKFAST, which we serve daily from 8:00 to 9:00. You can order DINNERS for your stay, which we serve from Monday to Thursday in the form of a two-course menu. From Friday to Sunday you can choose à la carte.
